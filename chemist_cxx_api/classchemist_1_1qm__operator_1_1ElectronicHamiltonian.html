Detailed Description

The subset of the Hamiltonian terms which depend on electrons.

In the electronic structure problem we are focused on Hamiltonian terms which depend on the electrons. The "electronic" Hamiltonian is the subset of the full Hamiltonian containing only terms that involve electrons.
