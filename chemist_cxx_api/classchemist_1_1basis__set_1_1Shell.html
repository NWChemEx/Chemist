Detailed Description

template<typename CGType>
class chemist::basis_set::Shell< CGType >

Models a set of AOs with a common center, the same total angular momentum, and the same contracted Gaussian function.

Each Cartesian AO is made up of the product of a contracted Gaussian function and a term of the form: such that (with being the total angular momentum of the AO. Spherical AOs are then formed by taking linear combinations of Cartesian AOs (the coefficients of the transformation being given by the spherical transform). Historically, in electronic structure theory we implicitly store much of this information in what is called a shell. A shell is comprised of a contracted Gaussian function, the total angular momentum, and whether the AOs in the shell are Cartesian or spherical (often als called pure). All other information is stored implicitly. At present, this class adheres to this convention.

CGTypeThe type of the contracted Gaussian common to all AOs in the shell.
