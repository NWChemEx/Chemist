template<typename... Particles>

Class describing a decomposable density.

In quantum mechanics, densities can be created from operators like the tensor representation of which in the basis looks like: where . The point being, when the transformation is known, the density decomposition in terms of the basis is also known. In many cases the basis is smaller and algorithms can exploit this fact to reduce the number of FLOPs that must be done by working with the factors instead of the density. This class contains the factors of the decomposition.

Following from the above discussion, this class establishes the convention that the matrix formed from the basis vectors of as column vectors is the left factor and that the matrix with the basis vectors of as the row vectors is the right factor.
